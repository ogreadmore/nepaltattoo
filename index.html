Our Story

Three Generations of Woodworking Excellence

Check out our services

Family Owned & Operated Since 1975

Founded by master craftsman Thomas Miller, Craftsman Woodworks began as a small workshop handcrafting custom furniture for local clients. Today, with Thomas's son James and grandson Daniel at the helm, we proudly carry forward a legacy of exceptional craftsmanship while embracing innovative techniques and environmentally responsible practices. Our skilled team blends time-honored woodworking knowledge passed down through generations with fresh, contemporary design sensibilities.
