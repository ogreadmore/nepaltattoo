Our Story

Making Jhapa Beautiful

Check out our services

Attention to detail is everything

Founded by master tattoo artist Anish Budhathoki, Feel Ink began as a humble studio in Jhapa, devoted to the art of tattooing and piercing. Today, Anish continues to guide the studio with his personal passion—seamlessly weaving Nepali cultural motifs with modern design flair and world-class hygiene standards.
